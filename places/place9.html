The Hanging Church

The church was given this name because it was built on two ancient towers from the towers of the famous Roman fort - Fort Babylon - which was built during the reign of Emperor Trajan.. The church was built at a height of 12 meters from the surface of the earth and on an area of 23 meters in length, 18.5 meters in width and 9.5 meters in height.. The church consists of 3 parts, which are the main nave and two small wings, including 8 columns on each side of the church.

What is the Best Time to Visit The Hanging Church ?

The church opens its doors to all visitors, seven days a week, from 9 am to 5 pm

How to Visit The Hanging Church?

The church is located in ancient Egypt, so if you come in your car, you can ride near the church, but if you come with transportation, the best way is to take the first line metro and get off the Margis station. You will get out of the station and you will find the church on the verge, and in less than a minute of walking you will be inside the church
