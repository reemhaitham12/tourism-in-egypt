Egyptian Pyramids

The Egyptian pyramids are ancient masonry structures located in Egypt. Sources cite at least 118 identified "Egyptian" pyramids. Approximately 80 pyramids were built within the Kingdom of Kush, now located in the modern country of Sudan.

What is the Best Time to Visit the Pyramids of Giza?

Winter ( October to March ) is the best time to visit Egypt. More specifically , the best time to visit the Pyramids of Giza is between 10 AM and noon after the morning rush, when youâ€™ll skip the crowds and also miss the hazy skies that prevent visibility. If you happen to visit in the summer months ( April to September ) try to visit in the morning to avoid the heat.

How to Visit the Pyramids of Giza?

To enter the Pyramids of Giza complex, you must first buy a ticket. There a many options:

  • Pyramids of Giza General Admission Tickets:  Adult: EGP 200 / Student: EGP 100
  • (This ticket does not cover entrance to the Great Pyramid, Pyramid of Khafre, Pyramid of Menkaure, Worker's Cemetery, or the Solar Boat Museum)
  • Pyramids of Giza Inclusive Area Tickets:  Adult: EGP 600 / Student: EGP 300
  • (This ticket includes entry to The Great Pyramid and the Solar Boat Museum)

You can also opt for comprehensive guided tours.
