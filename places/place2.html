Karnak Temple

Luxor is one of Egypt’s most precious inheritances when it comes to Egyptian ancient civilization. With a vast number of incredible ancient monuments, one of Luxor’s great temples is the Karnak Temple. The Karnak Temple Complex consists of a number of temples, chapels, and other buildings in the form of a village, and is for that reason that the name Karnak was given to this complex as in Arabic Karnak means ‘fortified village’.

What is the Best Time to Visit the karnak template?

Winter ( October to March ) is the best time to visit Egypt. More specifically , the best time to visit the karnak is between 10 AM and noon after the morning rush, when you’ll skip the crowds and also miss the hazy skies that prevent visibility. If you happen to visit in the summer months ( April to September ) try to visit in the morning to avoid the heat.

How to Visit the Karnak Template?

To enter the Karnak Template complex, you must first buy a ticket. There a many options:

  • Karnak Template General Admission Tickets:  Adult: EGP 250 / Student: EGP 300
  • (This ticket is only to visit the Karnak and thr road of "Elkepash")
  • Karnak Template Inclusive Area Tickets:  Adult: EGP 400 / Student: EGP 500
